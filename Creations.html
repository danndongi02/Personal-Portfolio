Project 1: CRM System

Technologies Used: Django, PostgreSQL

Description:

The primary goal of this CRM system was to demonstrate my ability to manage and handle large datasets, create a user-friendly interface, and seamlessly integrate frontend and backend systems through APIs. This project was a test of my skills in creating a robust and scalable database capable of managing extensive company data.
Key Features:
  • User-friendly dashboard for quick access to key metrics
  • Comprehensive contact management with advanced search and filter capabilities
  • Sales pipeline visualization to track and manage sales processes
  • Automated email reminders for follow-ups to enhance customer engagement
  • Detailed reporting and analytics for insightful business decisions
Personal Touch:

One of the biggest challenges was designing a database that could efficiently handle a large volume of data. Overcoming this obstacle not only strengthened my technical skills but also reinforced my problem-solving abilities.
